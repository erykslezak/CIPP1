Fire Alarms

Since starting in April 2006 we have teamed up with some of the leading manufacturers in the industry to provide the best in service, products and technology, All of our engineers are fully trained on all leading brands, with a wide experience our engineers can accomplish all your requirements.

Working with many of the biggest of companies in the fire alarms industry and have carried out works on some very extensive projects from hospitals, apartments, factories and comm’s centres providing everything from design through to commissioning.

We supply, install, commission, and repair all makes and types of fire alarm systems, from the most basic of systems to very complex systems together with our competitive prices and customer first approach this makes us one of the key fire systems suppliers in Ireland.

All our customers are covered with a full 24 hour emergency call out service should there be any problems.

Fire Safety

Fire Safety is of high importance and you cannot put a price on a life. At JMN Fire Alarms we can help you with any queries regarding your fire / life safety systems , we cater for upgrade / replacement of fire alarm systems and maintenance and servicing. With fire safety becoming more and more important it is critical that the equipment and the installation are up to the job and most importantly are correct for the applicable environment. That’s why at JMN fire alarms all our installations and equipment are installed to the European safety standards and IS 3218 Irish fire standards. Fire safety is and always should be number one.

Our Services

We provide a specialist fire safety service for life safety systems. Providing coverage across Leinster and providing a 24 hour emergency call out service to our customers. We can provide full sales, service, installation and training on all aspects of Fire Safety and Life Safety Systems, Emergency Lighting Systems, Fire Extinguishers, Suppression Systems and Smoke Ventilation Systems.

For more information on any or all of the above please contact us.
