JMN Suppression LTD supply, install and maintain all major brands of fire suppression systems and if its commercial or industrial we can supply the products, and the expertise to provide the solution to your needs.

Our engineers are all fully trained on all of our equipment and have many years of experience in the fire safety industry, for years we have been supplying top quality service and repairs to some of the biggest names in the fire safety business and customers all over Ireland.
